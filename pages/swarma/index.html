WEAR OS · FOURSQUARE SWARM

Check in from your wrist.

Swarma brings Swarm check-ins to your smartwatch. Find nearby places, check in, and earn sticker bonuses without reaching for your phone.

Join the beta For Pixel Watch, Galaxy Watch, and other Wear OS watches.

Lifelogging shouldn't interrupt life.

The check-in shouldn't interrupt the thing worth checking in about. Swarma keeps your hands free—open it and the places around you appear, ranked by where you actually go.

01

Just the venues, Ma'am

Venues are front and center on Swarma: no silly menus nor silly searching. A quick Refresh at the top pulls a fresh list when you've moved.

02

Your regular spots, first

Places you frequently check into effervesce to the top, and anywhere you've been before is highlighted, so your go-to spot is theoretically the first thing you see.

03

Stickers and bonuses

Add a Swarm sticker to any check-in and earn some sweet bonus points, right from your watch. Used stickers stay visible so you can always find the one you want.
